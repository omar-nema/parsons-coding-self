My dream home is...

Elusive
Building a home is a never-ending search. An established, fixed home feels unattainable.
Expansive
Home is constantly expanding as my experiences and relationships grow.
Amorphous
Home has no edges. It shifts and morphs gradually, taking on new forms.
Across time, home eludes, expands, and morphs. When I dream of home at this moment in time, I think:
Home is walls, constraints.
Home is windows, views into other lives.
Home is three-story houses, cracked pavement.
Home is wood, olive green.
Home is cardamom, sage.
Home is - tell me about your day.
Home is - let me help you.
Home is wajib, duty.
Home is purpose.
