This project contains a series of webpages that use personal data and interactive web graphics to convey what it feels like to live in - and be limited by - routine and structure.

Together, these visuals aim to give the viewer a sense of insularity, while also offering an honest, vivid picture of how I feel during an isolated, work-focused moment in time.
